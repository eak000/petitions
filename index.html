Find Your Voice

Be heard. Make a difference.

We the People is a part of www.whitehouse.gov. This site allows citizens to start petitions or add their signatures to existing petitions. Once a petition reaches 100,000 signatures, the White House must issue an official response. This is a great way for ordinary Americans to engage with their government on issues that matter to them.

Find an active petition on the issues that matter to you! Enter your search terms in the search box.
